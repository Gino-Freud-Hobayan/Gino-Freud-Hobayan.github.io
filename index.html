About me

Prior to my career as a data analyst, I was a consistent Academic scholar who graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Marine Transportation, where I got Licensed as a Merchant Marine Officer and worked onboard oil-chemical tankers as a Seafarer for almost 3 years.

When the COVID-19 pandemic put that career to a halt, I quickly adapted and acquired new skills, I then started working in the Financial Services industry and Sales for almost 2 years before becoming a Data analyst.

Currently, I've been working on building projects to further improve my skills in SQL, Python, Tableau, Excel, and other tools. I created several Capstone projects, Studied Statistics, and even completed the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate.

I've also been engaging in Weightlifting activities (Squats, Bench presses, and Deadlifts) as I believe there is a strong positive correlation between physical and mental health.
